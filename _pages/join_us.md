### Postdoc position in Tübingen (Germany, 3-5 years) 
Welcome! If you have a background (PhD) in machine learning, computer-science, neuroscience, physics, or related field and expertise in python, R, and/or another programming language(s) with experience in brain imaging (MRI, EEG, etc.), you are the ideal candidate for the advertised position! However, also send your CV, letter of motivation and contacts of two mentors if you do not reach the ideal entirely. The ideal is a high bar after all. Thus, if this position and our lab speaks to you, don’t hesitate to apply by [email](mailto:dr.thomas.wolfers@gmail.com). If you are the right person for the job, you will be placed on the German scale TV-L 13 at 100%, which is a very good salary compared internationally. The duration of your contract will be 3-5 years, which is dependent on the continuation of funding. Don’t hesitate to get in touch if you have any questions.
