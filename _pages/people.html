Thomas Wolfers, PI
Thomas holds a PhD in neuroscience and has been working as a Postdoc in Netherlands (Radboud University), researcher in Norway (University of Oslo), and visiting scholar in the United Kingdom (University of Oxford) since. In his work he uses and develops machine learning methods to understand the biology of individuals with mental health problems. Further, he is interested in finding better means for treatment of individual patients. He motivated to help people suffering from those conditions and to reduce stigma.
