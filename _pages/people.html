Milin Kim, PhD candidate
I am interested in the development of cerebellum over lifespan and its role in neurological and mental disorders. In my PhD, I am mapping the trajectory of the cerebellum using machine learning methods and brain imaging of large populations. In the long run, I hope my research will improve the lives of people with mental disorders. Before starting my PhD at Oslo University Hospital, I studied psychology at Seoul National University and continued with cognitive neuroscience at University of Oslo. Outside the lab, I spend my time exploring Norwegian forests and fjords, baking macarons, and reading.
